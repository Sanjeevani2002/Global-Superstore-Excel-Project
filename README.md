## **Executive Summary: Global Superstore Sales Dashboard**

### **1. Project Objective**

The objective of this project is to analyze the sales performance of a global retail superstore and provide actionable insights into revenue, profitability, customer trends, and product performance. The dashboard enables data-driven decision-making by identifying key growth areas and operational inefficiencies.

### **2. Dataset Source**

* **Link:** <a href=" ">Global Superstore</a>

### **3. Key Business Questions**

This dashboard addresses the following questions:

* What is the overall revenue and profit performance?
* How have sales and profits evolved over time?
* Which product categories contribute the most to revenue?
* What is the total customer base?
* How do discounts impact overall profitability?
* Which sub-categories drive the highest sales?

### **4. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**

* **Total Revenue:** 12.6 Million
* **Total Profit:** 1.5 Million
* **Number of Customers:** 51,290
* **Average Discount:** 14%

### **5. Dashboard Overview**

The dashboard provides an interactive and comprehensive view of business performance:

* **KPI Summary Section:** Displays key metrics such as revenue, profit, customers, and discount rate
* **Year-on-Year Sales Analysis:** Highlights steady growth in sales from 2011 to 2014
* **Year-on-Year Profit Analysis:** Shows consistent improvement in profitability
* **Category-wise Contribution:**

  * Technology: ~37.5%
  * Furniture: ~32.5%
  * Office Supplies: ~30%
* **Sub-category Performance Analysis:** Identifies top-performing and underperforming products
* **Interactive Filters:** Enables analysis by Year, Category, and Market

### **6. Process Followed**

1. **Data Collection**
   Imported the Global Superstore dataset from Kaggle

2. **Data Cleaning**

   * Removed duplicate records
   * Handled missing values
   * Standardized data formats

3. **Data Transformation**

   * Created calculated fields (Revenue, Profit, Discount %)
   * Aggregated data across different dimensions such as year, category, and region

4. **Data Analysis**

   * Performed trend analysis for sales and profit
   * Evaluated category-wise and sub-category performance
   * Analyzed customer distribution

5. **Dashboard Development**

   * Designed an interactive dashboard with charts and slicers
   * Ensured clarity, usability, and visual consistency

### **7. Key Insights**

* **Steady Business Growth:**
  Sales and profit have shown consistent upward trends from 2011 to 2014, indicating strong business expansion.

* **High Contribution from Technology Category:**
  Technology products contribute the highest share of total sales, making it the leading revenue-generating category.

* **Balanced Category Distribution:**
  All three categories contribute significantly, ensuring diversified revenue streams.

* **Impact of Discounts:**
  An average discount of 14% suggests a competitive pricing strategy, which requires monitoring to maintain profitability.

* **Variation in Sub-category Performance:**
  Significant differences exist among sub-categories, highlighting opportunities for optimization and strategic focus.

### **Conclusion**

The dashboard effectively highlights key performance trends and provides valuable insights into sales growth, profitability, and product performance. It supports strategic decision-making by identifying high-performing areas while also pointing out opportunities for improvement in discount management and product optimization.
